What is 挂刀 (Steam Balance Conversion)?

How It Works

Steam wallet balance can't be withdrawn directly. "挂刀" means buying items on the Steam Community Market, then selling them on third-party marketplaces (Buff163, Youpin, etc.) for real cash.

Reading the Ratio

Ratio = Third-Party Price / Steam Price. Lower is better. A ratio of 0.75 means you spend ¥100 on Steam and receive ¥75 in cash — a 25% conversion cost.

Fees Included

Net ratio accounts for Steam's 15% fee and each platform's seller fees. Steam takes 5% + 10%, Buff takes ~2.5%, Skinport takes 12%, CSFloat takes 2%.
